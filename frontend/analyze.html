How Confidence Is Evaluated
This assessment combines multiple forensic indicators rather than relying on a single test.

The analysis considers:

• Metadata completeness
• GPS provenance
• Camera consistency
• Compression behavior
• Structural consistency
• Synthetic image indicators

No automated analysis can guarantee authenticity.

This tool is intended to assist review and verification workflows by highlighting indicators that may support or reduce confidence in an image.
